Hiring

Wop, the new generation of freelancers.

 You are supported to develop a new way of working freely. Missions that make sense.

   I am available for a mission   
 Independent, employee, the best of both worlds! 
 Missions that make sense 

We work exclusively with NGOs, associations, projects of public, social, ecological interest, etc. All our projects are donated to Open Source.

 Predictable income

We pay you every month, immediately, a decent and fixed pre-established income. The advantages of self-employment and employment!

Public holidays and paid leave

We pay you for public holidays and days off! A visit to pay to the family? Want to spend the day walking? No worries, you get paid. Did you have that before as a freelancer?

Your costs covered

Are you a micro-entrepreneur and you pay charges on your expenses? Not with us. You simply spend your professional expenses with our company. And if you have to subcontract, no charge for you either!

1% to associations

We have decided to donate 1% of our profits each year to open source, humanitarian associations that contribute to a healthier world. To avoid biasing our choices, we are not asking for tax reductions.

How's it going ?

1. You are offered a mission

A first contact to present the mission to you. Together we find a monthly salary and a work pace that suits you. If you like it, let's go!

2. You work with the client

…without the commercial hassles and reminders. You focus on what you do best, and we do the rest.

3. You get paid

Is it the end of the month? We take stock, and your remuneration appears in just a few days.

FAQs

Who are the clients I will be working with?

 We have a strong particularity: we only take missions for NGOs, associations, public or general interest, free and open source projects. We reject proposals for commercial enterprises that do not meet our values, without exception.

I'm on a mission carried out by Wop, what does it consist of?

We are an umbrella company. Our commitment is to find missions and the best people who will fit this position. We take care of invoicing with the customer, your payment advances, we offer facilities for fees and charges, and many other new things!

What are the advantages compared to a micro-entrepreneur mission?

The advantages are multiple: - you only take care of your mission without the paperwork, without reminders - no more paying charges on your costs! we take care of your costs up front and can deduct them, so you avoid unjustified charges on these amounts.

Is there a difference between my remuneration and what the client pays?

We operate thanks to the missions on which we assign you to pay your costs and ours. So we can look for new missions, find accommodations, facilitate administrative tasks, participate in open source projects... and let you benefit from our advancements, find you a new mission and make your daily life even easier.

Do I have to charge VAT?

Whether or not you are subject to French VAT, your invoices must reach us excluding VAT.

My mission is finished, what will happen?

 It is very likely that we can renew your assignment or consider finding you a new assignment. Get in touch with us to advise on the best opportunity for you.

You find me a mission, can I invoice directly?

When you agree to go on an adventure with us, we take you to a mission and client presentation. Where you discover the new landscape that awaits you, it is because we have designed it for you, with upstream work, trust established with the client and negotiations to have good conditions. We have chosen you from among many potential contacts after a long period of research and expertise. This is why we are sure that the match is good. That’s our job, making sure it’s a favorite! We also work to respect the client's work, and yours.
